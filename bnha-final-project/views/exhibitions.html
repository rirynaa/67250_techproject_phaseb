Exhibitions

Aircraft Exhibition

This exhibition features a collection of aircrafts that highlight the history of aviation, technology, and the creativity that defines these aircrafts. Visitors can expect to see a wide range of aircraft models, aircraft parts that are unique to each era, and flying simulations.

Boat Exhibition

This exhibition showcases the evolution of boats and their impact on maritime culture across the world. Visitors will have the opportunity to test their ability to navigate the seas with cutting-edge simulations and interact with these fascinating vessels.
