Mission Statement

Here, at MonoMuse Museum, we are dedicated to spreading the love of learning by providing an engaging, inclusive, educational, and interactive experience for all of our visitors.


Milestones

  • 2018 - MonoMuse opens its doors
  • 2018 - First major exhibition: "Space Exploration"
  • 2021 - MonoMuse expands its collection to over 10,000 artifacts
  • 2022 - MonoMuse is awarded the "Best Contemporary Local DMV Museum" award.
  • 2024 - MonoMuse reaches 100,000 annual visitors
