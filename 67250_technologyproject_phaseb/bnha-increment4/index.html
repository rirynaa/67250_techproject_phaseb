Welcome to the MonoMuse Museum

MonoMuse museum is one of the largest natural history museums in the Washington, Maryland, and Virginia region, featuring an extensive collection of artifacts and exhibits.

Monomuse thrives on the community of art and technology enthusiasts. Read More

We are dedicated to providing an engaging and educational experience for visitors of all ages. Our exhibits cover a wide range of topics, including art, history, science, and culture. We also offer a variety of programs and events throughout the year, such as workshops, lectures, and family-friendly activities.
